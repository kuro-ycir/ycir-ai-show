Meet the Monsters

Witches

Witches are magical beings who use spells and potions. Often depicted riding brooms, they are a staple of folklore and fiction.

Vampires

Vampires are undead creatures that drink blood to survive. They are usually nocturnal and possess supernatural charm.

Zombies

Zombies are reanimated corpses that crave brains or human flesh. They are commonly featured in apocalyptic stories.

Aliens

Aliens are extraterrestrial beings from outer space. They come in all shapes and sizes, ranging from friendly visitors to dangerous invaders.

Monster Traits

Witch Traits
Spell-casting, broomstick flying, potion brewing
Vampire Traits
Immortality, blood-drinking, aversion to sunlight
Zombie Traits
Slow movement, insatiable hunger, mindlessness
Alien Traits
Advanced technology, unusual biology, otherworldly appearance
